The Me in Games

Besides my passion for music, I am also a game enthusiast.
I play many games, both PC and mobile. The types involved are also very diverse, including visual novels, puzzle solving, FPS, cards, tower defense, etc.
However, among various games, the one I spend the most time on is a game called Clash Royale.

Game Introduction

Clash Royale is a mobile game developed by Finnish game company Supercell, featuring real-time strategy, MOBA, and card elements. It was released on January 4, 2016. The game is based on the characters and worldview of Clash of Clans. In the game, players need to enhance their combat power by continuously opening treasure chests to obtain cards, and then engage in matching battles with other online players.

Me and this game...

I have been playing this game since the server was launched, and I have been playing it for 9 years now. I am also one of the oldest players of this game. At first, I played with my friends in elementary school, but now I am the only one who still insists on playing this game. In 9 years, apart from living and going to school, I can't think of anything else that would cost me more time than playing this game. Fortunately, I have also achieved some success in the game during these 9 years. My ranking on the ladder now is shown in the picture.
